Miami YAD is a branch of BLIA (Buddha's Light International Association) Fo Guang Shan Miami subchapter. YAD stands for "Young Adult Division", and it is as the name implies. We are a group that is led by only young adults, who are between the ages of 13-18. We do volunteer activities such as helping BLIA Miami with their events, or hosting our own events. We also do socials outside of volunteering, such as hanging out at malls, going to escape rooms, eating meals after meetings. * just a rough draft so it can be edited later *
